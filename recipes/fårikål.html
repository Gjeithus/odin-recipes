Fårikål

Description

Fårikål is the nation dish of Norway. It is part of the traditional dishes of Norway called "Husmansskost" It is usually served in early autumn corresponding with deliveries of fresh lambs meat. The english translation would be something like Mutton & Cabbage

Fårikål is very easy to make, but quite timeconsuming as it gets betteer if cooked over a long time. Enjoy this simple but delicious dish
