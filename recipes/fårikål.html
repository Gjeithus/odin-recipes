Fårikål

Description

Fårikål is the national dish of Norway. It is part of the traditional dishes of Norway called "Husmanskost" It is usually served in early autumn corresponding with deliveries of fresh lamb meat. The english translation would be something like Lamb & Cabbage

Fårikål is very easy to make, but quite timeconsuming as it gets betteer if cooked over a long time. Enjoy this simple but delicious dish

Ingredients

  • 1,5 kg Lamb stew meat
  • 1,5 kg Cabbage
  • 4 tsp Whole pepper
  • 2 tsp Salt
  • 3 dl Water

Steps

  1. Chop the cabbage into boats
  2. Layer lamb and cabbage in a pot, starting with lamb. Put pepper and and salt between the layers.
  3. Add water and bring to a boil
  4. Use a lid and loewer the heat.
  5. Cook until the meat loosens from the bone. About 2,5 hours
